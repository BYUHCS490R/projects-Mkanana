E Komo Mai

This site introduces key parts of Hawaiian culture and history, from traditional values like mālama ʻāina (caring for the land) to major events that reshaped the islands.

Use the navigation to explore cultural practices, learn about important historical moments, and share your own thoughts through a brief feedback form.

About This Site

The goal of this project is to highlight how Native Hawaiian culture developed over centuries of voyaging, settlement, and adaptation, and how it continues today despite colonization and political change.

Each page combines text, data, and media to show both the depth of Hawaiian traditions and the impact of events like the overthrow of the Hawaiian Kingdom and annexation by the United States.
